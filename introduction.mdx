VShop lets you check everything in your Valorant account without opening the game. It runs entirely on your device and communicates directly with Riot Games servers — no third-party backend is ever involved.

</Note>

## What VShop does

- **Daily shop** — view your rotating skin offers and their prices
- **Night Market** — check your discounted Night Market offers when active
- **Bundles** — browse featured bundle contents and pricing
- **Accessories** — see buddy, spray, player card, and title offers
- **Profile** — view your account progress, level, and currency balances

## How authentication works

VShop uses a Riot OAuth WebView embedded directly in the app. When you sign in, your credentials are transmitted from your device straight to `auth.riotgames.com` — the same endpoint the official Valorant client uses. VShop never sees, intercepts, or stores your password.

## Open source

VShop is fully open source. The source code is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/VShopApp/mobile). An Android APK is available for direct download from GitHub Releases — no app store required.
