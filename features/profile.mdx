The Profile screen gives you a summary of your Valorant account, including your display name, region, progression, and current currency balances.

## Account info

At the top of the screen, your **username** is shown alongside an avatar — a circle displaying the first two letters of your in-game name in uppercase.

Below that, the **Info** section shows:

| Field  | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Region | The Riot region your account is registered to (e.g. EU, NA, AP, KR) |

## Progression

The **Progress** section shows your current Valorant account level and XP:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Level | Your current account level |
| XP    | Your current XP within the active level |

## Currency balances

The **Balances** section shows all four currency types on your account:

| Currency | Full name | Used for |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| VP | Valorant Points | Purchasing skins, bundles, and Night Market items |
| Radianite | Radianite Points | Upgrading owned skin levels and variants |
| KC | Kingdom Credits | Purchasing items in the Accessory Shop |
| Free Agent | Free Agent currency | Unlocking agents |

<Tip>
Your VP, KC, and Radianite balances are fetched live from the Riot wallet API each time you open the app, so they always reflect your actual in-game totals.
</Tip>
