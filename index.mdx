## What is VShop?

VShop is an open-source React Native mobile app that lets Valorant players browse their daily skin shop, Night Market, featured bundles, and accessory store — all without opening the game client. It runs entirely on your device and communicates directly with Riot Games' official servers.

## Privacy & security

VShop authenticates directly with Riot Games' official OAuth servers — your credentials never touch any third-party server. Authentication tokens are stored exclusively on-device using secure cookie storage.
