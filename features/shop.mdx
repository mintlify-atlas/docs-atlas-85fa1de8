The Daily Shop shows the 4 weapon skins currently available for purchase in your Valorant account. The shop rotates every day, so VShop displays a live countdown timer so you always know when the next refresh is.

## What you see

Each skin offer shows:

- **Skin name** — with a star prefix (`⭐`) if the skin is on your wishlist
- **VP price** — the cost in Valorant Points
- **Skin artwork** — the display icon or full render of the skin
- **Levels button** — tap to preview all upgrade levels (video or icon)
- **Chromas button** — tap to preview all color variants (video or full render)

A countdown timer in the top-right corner shows exactly how long remains until the shop resets (formatted as `HH:MM:SS`, or `D:HH:MM:SS` when more than 24 hours remain).

## Data source

Shop data comes from Riot's storefront API. The 4 daily offers are sourced from the `SkinsPanelLayout.SingleItemStoreOffers` field of the storefront response. The reset timer is derived from `SingleItemOffersRemainingDurationInSeconds`.

## Wishlist integration

If you have added a skin to your wishlist, it appears at the top of its card with a star (`⭐`) next to the name, making it easy to spot at a glance when a wishlisted skin is in your daily rotation.

To add or remove a skin from your wishlist, go to the [Wishlist](/features/wishlist) screen and manage your skins there.

<Tip>
If you enable wishlist notifications, VShop will alert you automatically when a wishlisted skin appears in your daily shop — no need to check manually.
</Tip>
