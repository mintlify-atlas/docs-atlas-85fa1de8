The Accessory Shop offers a rotating selection of non-weapon cosmetics purchasable with **Kingdom Credits (KC)**. It refreshes on the same schedule as the Daily Shop and features up to 4 accessory items at a time.

A countdown timer in the top-right corner shows when the current accessory offers expire.

## Accessory types

The Accessory Shop can contain four different types of cosmetics. Each type is displayed differently in VShop:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Gun buddies">
    Gun buddies are small charms that attach to your weapon. They are displayed with a small icon image.

    VShop shows the buddy's display icon sourced from the buddy level's `displayIcon` field.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Player cards">
    Player cards are the banner artwork shown on your profile and in loading screens. They are displayed with their large art format.

    VShop renders player cards using the `largeArt` field, giving you a clear preview of the full card design.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sprays">
    Sprays are graphics you can apply to surfaces in-game. They are displayed using the full transparent icon.

    VShop shows sprays using the `fullTransparentIcon` field.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Player titles">
    Player titles are short text strings displayed beneath your name. Because they are text-only, no image is shown.

    VShop displays the title's display name as a text card with no artwork.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Pricing

All accessory items are priced in **Kingdom Credits (KC)**, not Valorant Points. The price is shown on each item card next to the KC currency icon.

## Data source

Accessory data comes from the `AccessoryStore.AccessoryStoreOffers` field in the Riot storefront API response. Prices use the `KC` currency from each offer's `Cost` field. The reset timer is sourced from `AccessoryStoreRemainingDurationInSeconds`.
