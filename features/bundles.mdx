The Featured Bundles screen shows the weapon skin bundles currently available in the Valorant store. Bundles group multiple skins (and sometimes other cosmetics) together, often at a discount compared to buying items individually.

## What you see

Each bundle displays:

- **Bundle artwork** — a full-width banner image representing the bundle's visual theme
- **Bundle name and total price** — the combined discounted price for all items in the bundle (in VP)
- **Countdown timer** — shows how long the bundle is available before it rotates out
- **Individual skin cards** — each weapon skin included in the bundle is listed separately

Each individual skin card within a bundle shows:

- **Skin name**
- **Separate purchase price** — the VP cost if you were to buy this skin outside the bundle
- **Skin artwork**
- **Levels and Chromas buttons** — tap to preview upgrade levels and color variants

## Bundle pricing

The total bundle price shown is calculated as the sum of all individual items' discounted prices (`DiscountedPrice`) from the Riot API. The per-item price displayed on each card is the base price (`BasePrice`) — what the item costs when purchased separately.

<Note>
Only weapon skin levels (`SkinLevel` item type) are shown in the bundle item list. Other cosmetic types included in a bundle (such as gun buddies or player cards) are not individually listed.
</Note>

## Data source

Bundle data comes from the `FeaturedBundle.Bundles` field in the Riot storefront API response. Bundle artwork and metadata are fetched from the Valorant assets API using the bundle's `DataAssetID`. The per-bundle countdown uses `DurationRemainingInSeconds`.
